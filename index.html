Our Story

Founded on a Vision of Collective Success

EmpowerHer Collective was born from a simple yet powerful idea: that women achieve more when they work together. Our founder, [Founder's Name], experienced firsthand the unique challenges female entrepreneurs face. She envisioned a central hub—a digital ecosystem—where ambition meets opportunity.

Launched in 2022, we started as a small directory and have since blossomed into a global movement, touching thousands of founders across dozens of industries. Our commitment remains the same: to provide a platform that champions, supports, and elevates women in business.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is eligible to join the directory?

Our directory is open to all self-identifying female founders who are the primary decision-makers in their registered business.

What are the benefits of the premium plan?

Premium members receive a featured profile, access to our full resource library, exclusive event invitations, and opportunities to be highlighted in our newsletter and social media channels.

How do I apply?

Simply choose a plan from our Plans & Pricing page and click "Join Now" to fill out the application form. Our team will review your submission within 3-5 business days.
